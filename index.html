Introduction

This application allows you to view traffic images captured from various parts of Singapore, and check for the rainfall values across Singapore on certain dates and timings.

After entering the date and time and clicking on the "Search" button, icons will appear on the Google Map. Click on the icons on the map to view the details, and scroll down to view the traffic images (only after searching for Traffic condition). Clicking on the "Clear Results" button will remove the respective search results.
